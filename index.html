Crystals Collector

Let's play crystals collector! The goal is to add up your score to the target number.

When the game starts, each of the four crystals will be randomly assigned a value. The value changes at the start of each game.

When you click on a crystal, you will add that crystal's value to your total score. The value of each crystal will remaim hidden from you, so pay close attention to your score!

To win the game, you must match your score to the target number. If your total total score goes above the target number, you lose.

Click a crystal to start the game!
