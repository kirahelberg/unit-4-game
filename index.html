Crystals Collector

Let's play crystals collector! The goal is to add up your score to the target number.

There are four crystals below. When the game starts, each crystal will be randomly assigned a value. The value changes at the start of each game.

By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. The value of each crystal will remain hidden from you, so pay close attention to your score!

You win the game by matching your total score to the target number, and you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Click a crystal to start the game!
